Purpose

The author component identifies the post author and gives readers a short biography with social links.

Example

Author: Colin Bacon

Colin Bacon is a professional development engineer, with a passion for C# and creating compelling frontend experiences with HTML and CSS. In his free time he can be found teaching skiing at his local slope.

  • Twitter

Usage notes

  • Use the component near article footers or profile summaries.
  • Wrap the component in a colour utility when it appears on a dark background.
  • Use .c-author-links for supporting social profile links.

Accessibility notes

  • Provide useful alt text for the author image.
  • Use rel="author" on the author profile link.
  • Keep social link text available to screen readers when using icon or hidden-text styles.
